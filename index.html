LexiLoop

About

Welcome to LexiLoop! I'm excited that you're here! If you enter a word which is listed in your normal dictionary into the box below, you'll see several tracks appear to create your sequencer, aka looper! However, instead of using boring drum samples, we are using text-to-speech for each syllable! Play and reorder the syllables from your favorite words! You may notice some of the syllables may be pronounced incorrectly, but that's just part of the fun!
