LexiLoop

About

Welcome to LexiLoop! I'm excited that you're here! If you enter a word into that box which is listed in your normal dictionary, you'll see several tracks appear to create your sequencer, aka looper! Instead of using drum samples we are using text-to-speech for each syllable! Mix and match the syllables from your favorite words. Some of the syllables may be pronounced incorrectly, but that's just part of the fun!
